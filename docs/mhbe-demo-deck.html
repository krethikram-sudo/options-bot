What Outlay does

Three things, from data you already have.

  • Attribute — every AI dollar mapped to a team, work type, ticket, and engineer.
  • Forecast — what planned work will cost, back-tested on your own delivered work.
  • Govern — budgets per program, real-time pacing, and on-/off-track alerts.

How it connects

Read-only links to your work tracker (Jira / GitHub / Linear) and your AI provider’s usage & billing API. No agents installed, nothing in the request path.

Why this is safe for a health exchange

We never see PHI, PII, or FTI — by design.

  • Metadata-only — token counts, ticket IDs, work types, dollar figures. Never prompts or model outputs.
  • Bring-your-own-key — your API keys stay in your environment.
  • Read-only — Outlay observes; it’s not a proxy or gateway in any data path.

What that means for your review

None of the data MARS-E / ARC-AMPE, IRS Pub 1075, or HIPAA govern ever reaches us. The integration sits outside that data scope — which is exactly what stops most vendors at the door.
