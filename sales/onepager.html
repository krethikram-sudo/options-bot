Map your AI spend to your roadmap — in two weeks.

Outlay turns AI compute from an unpredictable, runaway line item into something you budget and govern like any other engineering cost. Your AI bill is climbing and now finance-visible — but it lives in API dashboards keyed by model, not the work that drove it. No one can say what an epic costs, forecast next quarter from the roadmap, or get warned before a team blows its budget. Outlay fixes exactly that.

Privacy by architecture. Outlay runs on metadata only — task categories, token counts, ticket IDs. Prompt text, model outputs, and API keys never leave your environment, and our ingestion endpoints reject any payload that contains them. The boundary is enforced, not just promised.
