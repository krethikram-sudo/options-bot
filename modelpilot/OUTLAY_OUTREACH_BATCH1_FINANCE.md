> **Positioning: Outlay turns AI spend into a finance artifact** — COGS attribution by product/team,

> metadata-only, no proxy. Links: tour `https://outlay-ai.com/tour` · pilot `https://app.outlay-ai.com/pilot-request`.

## The motion: finance-led, eng-implemented
1. **Open with finance** (CFO / VP Finance / Head of Finance / FinOps / Head of Strategic Finance). They
   feel the pain and can say "yes, this matters." Lead with margin/forecast/board — not tickets/engineers.
2. **Lower the perceived lift:** the only eng ask is **~30 min to connect two read-only tokens** — no code
   change, nothing in the request path. Say this explicitly; it's what makes finance comfortable saying yes.
3. **Then loop eng** for the connect (the eng-persona drafts in the companion file are the second thread).
4. **Multi-thread when you can:** email finance, and either CC or separately ping the eng lead the same
   week referencing the finance conversation. Two coordinated touches > one.

## What lands with finance (use these hooks, not the eng ones)
- **AI is becoming a COGS line you can't forecast** → margin surprises, bad budgets.
- **No attribution by product/team** → you can't do unit economics or defend pricing.
- **Board/investor reporting on AI gross margin** is now a question you get asked.
- **Defensible numbers:** forecasts are back-tested on your own closed work and shown with the sample size.
- **Clears the compliance reflex:** metadata only — prompts/outputs/keys never leave your environment.
