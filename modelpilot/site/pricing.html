Pricing

Priced on the spend we govern — not per seat.

Your value from Outlay scales with your AI bill, so that's what we price on: a flat platform fee on the AI spend we attribute and govern. Plus an optional savings-share where we only bill on savings we can measure — and unlike the cloud optimizers, no clawback. The exact number is scoped to your spend in a short consultation; here's exactly how it works.

How pricing works

Primary
Platform fee on spend under management

A predictable flat band based on the annual AI spend Outlay watches. The % falls as your spend grows, so you're never penalized for scaling. No per-seat fees — add as many viewers as you want.

Optional add-on
Savings-share, measured

Turn on enforcement or take a commitment we sized, and we bill 20% of the savings we can prove against a held-out control arm. No clawback, no lock-in — cancel anytime and keep the savings.

The savings-share, in plain terms.

If you enable the opt-in gateway or act on a commitment we recommended, we measure the savings against a held-out control arm and bill 20% of what we can prove — billed in arrears, on realized savings only. No early-termination penalty and no clawback of unrealized share (a friction the cloud optimizers charge for). If we don't save you money, you don't pay the share. It's how we put our money where our mouth is.
