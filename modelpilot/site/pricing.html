Optional: pay on outcomes, not seats.

Turn on the hard-cap gateway or act on a procurement mix we sized, and you can swap the per-person fee for 20% of the savings we can prove against a held-out control arm — billed in arrears, on realized savings only. No clawback, no lock-in. If we don't save you money, you don't pay the share. It's how we put our money where our mouth is.

What counts as a "tracked person"?

Anyone whose AI usage Outlay attributes in a billing month — the people behind the tickets, branches, and sessions we map spend to. Viewers, admins, and finance seats are free and unlimited; you only pay for the people actually driving AI spend. Nobody's prompts are ever read.

Is there really no per-usage or per-token fee?

Correct. Team is a flat price per tracked person, full stop — attribution volume, forecast runs, and budget checks don't meter. Your bill is predictable no matter how much your teams build.

How does the outcome add-on get measured?

When you enable enforcement or take a commitment we recommended, we hold out a control arm and compare, so the savings figure is measured on your own traffic — not asserted. We bill 20% of what we can prove, in arrears. There's no clawback of unrealized share and no early-termination penalty.

Do you offer government / education pricing?

Yes — Enterprise covers US state & local government and education, with the compliance evidence (SOC 2 / StateRAMP path), a DPA, and the metadata-only, BYOK, read-only architecture that keeps citizen and customer data out of scope entirely. See the security overview →
