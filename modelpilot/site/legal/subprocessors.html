Subprocessors

The third-party services ModelPilot relies on to deliver the product. We'll keep this page current and give notice of material changes.

Important: your prompt content goes directly from your infrastructure to Anthropic using your own API key — it does not pass through ModelPilot. Anthropic is therefore your model provider, not ModelPilot's subprocessor for prompt content. The services below process only account data and aggregate/opt-in metadata.
