Healthcare

Govern your Claude spend — without PHI ever leaving your environment.

Health-tech teams can't route patient data through a third-party gateway. Outlay is built so it never has to: prompts, model outputs, and your API key never reach us — we read usage metadata only, and we're not in the path of your AI calls.

Why this works for healthcare: Outlay connects read-only to your tracker and your provider's usage data, and only a task category + numeric features (token counts, ticket IDs) are used to attribute spend. Prompt text and completions — where PHI lives — never leave your environment; Outlay is not a proxy and isn't in the path of your AI calls.

What that means for your security & compliance review

✓No PHI egress to Outlay

The sensitive data physically can't reach us — we read usage metadata and ticket references, never request or response bodies.

✓You keep your own Anthropic relationship

Your calls go to Anthropic directly with your key; Outlay isn't a proxy and isn't in the path of your prompt content. Configure Anthropic's data terms (e.g. zero-retention) with them as you do today.

✓Read-only by design

Connect with read-only tokens; remove Outlay anytime and nothing about how your calls are made changes — it was never in the path.

✓Defensible forecasts

Cost forecasts are back-tested on your own closed tickets (leave-one-out) and shown with the sample size — a number you can defend internally.

The honest part on certifications

Our PHI-never-leaves-your-environment guarantee is a property of the architecture, verifiable in the (inspectable) client. We are not yet SOC‑2 or HIPAA certified, and we won't claim what we don't hold. If you need a BAA, a security questionnaire, or our compliance roadmap, email us — we'll share exactly where we are.
