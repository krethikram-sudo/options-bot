Govern your Claude spend — without PHI ever leaving your environment.

Health-tech teams can't route patient data through a third-party gateway. Outlay is built so it never has to: prompts, model outputs, and your API key never reach us.

Why this works for healthcare: Outlay connects read-only to your tracker and your provider's usage data, and only a task category + numeric features (token counts, ticket IDs) are used to attribute spend. Prompt text and completions — where PHI lives — never leave your environment; Outlay is not in the path of your AI calls.

What that means for your security & compliance review

  • No PHI egress to Outlay. The sensitive data physically can't reach us — we read usage metadata and ticket references, never request or response bodies.
  • You keep your own Anthropic relationship. Your calls go to Anthropic directly with your key; Outlay isn't a proxy and isn't in the path of your prompt content. Configure Anthropic's data terms (e.g. zero-retention) with them as you do today.
  • Read-only by design. Connect with read-only tokens; remove Outlay anytime and nothing about how your calls are made changes — it was never in the path.
  • Defensible forecasts. Cost forecasts are back-tested on your own closed tickets (leave-one-out) and shown with the sample size — a number you can defend internally.
